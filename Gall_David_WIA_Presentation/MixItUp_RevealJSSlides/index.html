Eye Catching Homepage

As soon as you land on the homepage there is a working example that you can try. Once I tried it on the site I wanted to actually try it. This is the homepage.

Several Uses Come to Mind

When I saw this plugin, I thought that it would be great for an image gallery or to sort a list of selected items.

It's Responsive

Easy as Pie 'They say'

It's actually true it was quite simple. Just follow the step by step instructions and bingo you have some cool graphics for your gallery.
